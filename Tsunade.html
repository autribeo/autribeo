Tsunade


1. Superhuman Strength


  • Tsunade possesses monstrous physical strength due to her precise chakra control. By focusing chakra into her fists or feet, she can unleash devastating punches and kicks that can shatter the ground, send enemies flying, or even destroy entire buildings.

2. Byakugō Seal (Strength of a Hundred Seal)


  • A diamond-shaped seal on her forehead that stores chakra over time. When released, it grants her immense healing abilities, making her nearly invulnerable in battle. This also enhances her physical abilities and chakra reserves significantly.

3. Summoning Jutsu: Katsuyu


  • A powerful downward kick that creates a massive crater upon impact. This technique is strong enough to obliterate anything in its path.
