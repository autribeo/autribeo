Sasuke


1. Eternal Mangekyō Sharingan


  • After awakening the Mangekyō Sharingan, Sasuke gained access to powerful techniques:

  • Amaterasu

  • Black flames that burn anything in their path and never extinguish until the target is completely destroyed

  • Kagutsuch

  • A unique ability that lets Sasuke control and shape the Amaterasu flames into weapons.

  • Susanoo

  • A massive chakra warrior that grants nearly invincible offense and defense.

  • Genjutsu: Sharingan

  • Allows him to paralyze or manipulate opponents instantly.

2. Susanoo (須佐能乎)


  • Sasuke’s Susanoo evolved into a nearly invincible warrior with advanced offensive and defensive abilities

  • His Perfect Susanoo was strong enough to rival even the strongest shinobi.

3. Rinnegan


  • After receiving Hagoromo Ōtsutsuki’s power, Sasuke awakened the Rinnegan, granting him god-like abilities:

  • Amenotejikara

  • A space-time jutsu that lets Sasuke instantly swap places with objects or people.

  • Six Paths Techniques

  • Access to powerful jutsu like absorbing chakra, summoning creatures, and controlling gravity.

4. Chidori & Lightning Style Techniques


  • Chidori

  • A powerful lightning-based thrust capable of piercing through almost anything.

  • Kirin

  • A devastating lightning strike that Sasuke summons from real thunderstorms, capable of obliterating an entire battlefield.

  • Chidori Nagashi

  • Releases lightning from his body, shocking and paralyzing nearby enemies.

4. Indra’s Arrow


  • Sasuke’s most powerful attack while using his Perfect Susanoo.

  • A giant arrow infused with chakra and lightning, strong enough to match Naruto’s Six Paths: Ultra Big Ball Rasenshuriken.
