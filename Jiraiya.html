Jiraiya


1 Sage Mode


  • Jiraiya’s most powerful transformation, allowing him to harness Senjutsu chakra, which enhances his strength, speed, and durability.

  • Unlike Naruto’s perfect Sage Mode, Jiraiya’s transformation is incomplete, causing slight toad-like features to appear on his face

  • To maintain this form, he summons Fukasaku and Shima, two elder toads who help him gather natural energy while also providing powerful support techniques like genjutsu.

2. Rasengan


  • A high-level A-rank jutsu created by Minato Namikaze, Jiraiya’s student.

  • Jiraiya mastered and improved this technique, teaching it to Naruto.

  • He later developed Ultra-Big Ball Rasengan, a much larger and more destructive version of the original.

3. Summoning Jutsu (Toads of Mount Myoboku)


  • Jiraiya forms a contract with the toads, allowing him to summon them in battle.

  • His summons provide both offensive power and strategic advantages, making them key to his fighting style.

  • His most notable summons include:

  • A massive toad wielding a giant sword, capable of spitting oil for Fire Release attacks.

  • Fukasaku & Shima – Two elder toads who assist in Sage Mode and use powerful genjutsu.

  • Gamaken – A strong but clumsy toad wielding a shield and sasumata (a pole weapon).
